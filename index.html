Price

Our Set4 offers competitive pricing for a variety of editing services. Here's an overview of our pricing structure:

  1. Document Editing:
    • Proofreading: Starting at $0.03 per word
    • Copyediting: Starting at $0.05 per word
    • Substantive Editing: Starting at $0.08 per word
  2. Photo Editing:
    • Basic Retouching: Starting at $5 per photo
    • Advanced Editing (including background removal and color correction): Starting at $10 per photo
  3. Video Editing:
    • Basic Editing (trimming, transitions, and basic effects): Starting at $50 per hour of footage
    • Advanced Editing (complex effects, audio enhancements): Starting at $100 per hour of footage
  4. Resume Editing:
    • Basic Editing: Starting at $25 per resume
    • Advanced Editing (including reformatting and restructuring): Starting at $50 per resume
  5. PDF Editing:
    • Basic Editing (text and layout changes): Starting at $10 per PDF
    • Advanced Editing (graphic design changes, form creation): Starting at $25 per PDF
Please note that these are starting prices and the final cost may vary depending on the complexity of the project. For a detailed quote based on your specific requirements, please contact us with details about your project, and we'll provide you with a personalized price estimate.
